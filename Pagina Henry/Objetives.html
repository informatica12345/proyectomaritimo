OBJECTIVES

1. By collecting garbage we tend to reduce pollution since plastics decompose over time and produce toxic substances that affect the quality of water and marine life.



2. To demonstrate to people that technology if used for a good purpose can be beneficial.



3. Protection of human health since contamination produces diseases such as cholera, other diarrheal diseases, dysentery, hepatitis A, typhoid fever and poliomyelitis.



4. An important part of our project is to help and seek to restore damaged reef ecosystems by breeding and transplanting corals. This project will not only help to recover marine biodiversity but will also protect the sea from the pollution it suffers today.
